CIIPro Datasets

There are training sets available for three different complex toxicological endpoints:

  • Acute Oral Toxicity
  • Hepatotoxicity
  • Ocular Toxicity

Acute Oral Toxicity

This dataset contain 8,807 chemically-diverse compounds with LD50 values from the rat acute oral toxicity test. The values span a wide range from 0.012 mg/kg to 64,500 mg/kg. They have been curated and stem from a variety of reliable sources including the National Toxicology Program's Interagency Center for the Evaluation of Alternative Toxicological Methods (NICEATM), ChemIDplus, the Hazardous Substances Data Bank, European Chemical Agency and the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA). Compounds with an LD50 values less than 1,000 mg/kg are considered toxic in this dataset.
